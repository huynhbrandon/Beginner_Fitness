Exercises

Push ups

  • Targets the chest,shoulder, and tricep muscles
  • Engage lower back and core muscles for stability
  • No equipment!

Pull ups

  • Targets the back, core, bicep, and shoulder muscles
  • Keep entire body tense throughout entire movement
  • Pull all the way up, release all the way down for full range of motion

Body Squats

  • Targets the thigh(quads,hamstrings), glutes, and core muscles
  • Lower body exercises utilize more muscles thus burning more calories
  • Go down as low as possible, while preventing curvature of the lower back
