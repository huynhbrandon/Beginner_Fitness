Meal Plans and Strategies

Ketogenic Diet

  • Low-carb, high-fat diet
  • Reduction in carbs puts your body in ketosis
  • Ketosis allows for burning fat for energy
  • Foods to avoid:
    • Soda, fruit juice, smoothies
    • Rice, pasta, cereal
    • Unhealthy fats such as processed vegetable oils, mayonnaise
  • Sample keto plan

Paleo Diet

  • Foods similar to what might have been eaten during the Paleolithic era
  • Lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts and seeds
  • Limits foods that became common when farming emerged (dairy products, legumes, grains...)
  • Foods to avoid:
    • Dairy products
    • Legumes, such as beans, lentils, peanuts
    • Potatoes
  • Sample paleo plan

Weight Gain Diet

  • Obesity is currently one of the world's biggest health problems, but being underweight may be just as harmful
  • Calorie surplus is key (Eat more calories than your body burns)
  • Easy to fall into the pit of binging any and all foods, but this quickly opens the flood gates to unhealthy habits
  • Keep protein intake high
